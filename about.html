About Me

Discover my coding journey, passion for technology, and why I love creating digital projects

Let's build something!

Hello! And welcome to my page. I'm a shade-tree developer who loves creating digital projects in my free time. Coding is my creative outlet where I can bring ideas to life.

I enjoy experimenting with different technologies such as Swift for iOS and macOS apps, HTML/CSS for web projects, and PowerShell for automation scripts.

My Coding Journey

My journey into coding began several years ago. I had experience with writing scripts using powershell, but I was always interested in Swift and creating apps for iOS and macOS. When I was laid off at the beginning of the pandemic, I decided to use this time to dive deeper into Swift development and explore my passion for creating applications.

What began as a casual interest has grown into a fulfilling hobby that allows me to solve problems, create useful tools, and express my creativity. My first Swift app was a travel app to list locations you've visited.

Why I Code

For me, coding is about:

  • Problem-solving: I enjoy the challenge of figuring out how to make things work
  • Creativity: Turning ideas into functional applications is incredibly satisfying
  • Learning: Technology is always evolving, and there's always something new to discover
  • Utility: Creating tools that make life easier or more organized

My Approach

I believe in learning by doing. Each project I undertake teaches me something new, whether it's a better way to structure code, a new framework feature, or a more efficient algorithm.

I'm not a professional developer - coding is purely a hobby for me. This means I can explore technologies that interest me without pressure, and I can take my time to understand concepts deeply.
