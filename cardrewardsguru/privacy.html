Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

1. Information We Collect

Card Rewards Guru is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal information to provide our services:

Personal Information

  • Email address (for account creation and support)
  • Name (optional, for personalization)
  • Device information (for app optimization)

Financial Information

  • Credit card information (stored locally on your device)
  • Rewards balances (stored locally or with your permission)
  • Spending categories (for rewards optimization)
Important: Your credit card details are never transmitted to our servers. All sensitive financial information is stored locally on your device using industry-standard encryption.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Personalize your experience
  • Send you important updates and notifications
  • Improve our app and develop new features
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Detect and prevent fraud

3. Data Security

We implement security measures designed to protect your information:

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • Local storage for sensitive financial information
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Secure server infrastructure with enterprise-grade protection

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services that collect information to help us operate our service:

  • Analytics: Google Analytics (anonymous usage data)
  • Crash Reporting: Firebase Crashlytics
  • Cloud Services: AWS for backup and synchronization (optional)

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@example.com.

6. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
