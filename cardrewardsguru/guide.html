Using My Cards

This guide walks you through the main features of the app based on the current UI and code.

Adding Your First Card

  1. Open the app. On the My Cards screen, tap + or Add Credit Card.
  2. Choose how to add a card:
    • Scan with Camera to capture details automatically.
    • Manual Entry to type in details yourself.
  3. If you reach the free limit, you'll be prompted to upgrade to add more cards.

Viewing and Editing Cards

  • Tap any card in the list to view details or edit it.
  • Swipe left on a card to quickly Edit or Delete.
  • Cards nearing renewal are highlighted and may show a Renewal soon badge.

Renewal Highlights

The app highlights upcoming renewals within 30 days. If a card has a specific renewal date, that date is used. If it has only a renewal month (e.g., "September"), the next occurrence of that month is used.

Tip: You can set a renewal date or month in the card details screen.

Rewards and Annual Fees

  • Each card can have multiple reward categories. The best category is shown on the list.
  • If a card has an annual fee, it is displayed along with the renewal information.

Premium Subscription

Free users can add up to 3 cards. Upgrade to premium to add more. You can manage your subscription from the upgrade prompt.
