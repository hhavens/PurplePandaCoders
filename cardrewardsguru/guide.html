Using My Cards

This guide walks you through the main features of the app based on the current UI and code.

Getting Started

1 Adding Your First Card

  1. Open the app. On the My Cards screen, tap +.
  2. Choose how to add a card:
    • Scan with Camera to capture details automatically.
      Note: Darker cards do not scan well. You might still need to enter details manually.
    • Manual Entry to type in details yourself.
  3. If you reach the free limit, you'll be prompted to upgrade to add more cards.
Note: Templates have preconfigured rewards and annual rates. You can customize these after adding the card.

2 Viewing and Editing Cards

  • Tap any card in the list to view details or edit it.
  • Swipe left slowly (as to not accidentally delete) on a card to quickly Edit or Delete.
  • Cards nearing renewal are highlighted and may show a Renewal soon badge.

3 Viewing Recommendations

  • Select the Recommendations tab.
  • View the card recommendation for the location you are currently at.
  • Scroll to bottom to search for a recommendation. Used for websites, such as travel, where the app cannot pick up your location.

Key Features

Renewal Highlights

The app highlights upcoming renewals within 30 days. If a card has a specific renewal date, that date is used. If it has only a renewal month, the next occurrence of that month is used.

Rewards Optimization

Each card can have multiple reward categories. The best category is shown on the list. Select the card that has the best rewards and maximize your benefits.

Recommendations

Get recommendations for the best cards based on your location.

Tip: You can set a renewal date or month in the card details screen to get timely reminders.

Premium Subscription

Free users can add up to 3 cards. Upgrade to premium to add unlimited cards and access advanced features. You can manage your subscription from the upgrade prompt or in your account settings.

Premium Benefits:

  • Unlimited credit cards
  • Annual renewal date field
  • Annual renewal fee field
  • Annual renewal reminders
  • All credit card templates
  • Ad-free experience
