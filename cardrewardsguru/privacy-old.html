Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy describes how the My Cards app ("the App", "we", "us") handles your information.

Data We Collect

  • On-device card data: Card names, types, last four digits, rewards, annual fee, and renewal information you enter or scan.
  • Subscription status: Whether you have a premium subscription, stored locally and/or retrieved via App Store mechanisms.

How We Use Data

  • To display and manage your cards within the App.
  • To determine eligibility for features (e.g., card limits for free vs premium).
  • To highlight upcoming renewals.

Data Storage

All card data is stored on your device. We do not transmit your card data to our servers. If you enable platform services (e.g., iCloud backup), your data may be backed up according to your system settings.

Third-Party Services

The App may use Apple services such as StoreKit for subscriptions and system frameworks for camera scanning. These services are subject to Apple's terms and privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Choices

  • You can delete cards at any time within the App.
  • You can opt not to upgrade to premium; the App remains usable with free limits.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact privacy@example.com.
