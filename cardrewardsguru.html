Maximize Your Credit Card Benefits

CardRewardsGuru is my passion project that helps people make the most of their credit card rewards and travel benefits. As an avid points enthusiast, I created this tool to simplify the complex world of credit card rewards.

The application analyzes your spending patterns and recommends the best credit cards to maximize your rewards in different categories like travel, dining, groceries, and more.

Built with a combination of web technologies and data analysis, CardRewardsGuru represents my interest in both coding and personal finance optimization.
